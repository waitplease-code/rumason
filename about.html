About Us

Resources Unlimited Masonry is a masonry restoration company owned and operated by Richard Williams who has over forty years of experience in the construction industry. He started as a laborer right after high school working for local building contractors and worked his way up to become a masonry contractor. His experience and knowledge gained through years of working on various construction projects created a foundation for the development of his own building and masonry restoration business. His exceptional leadership qualities are proven by the high quality work and many satisfied clients. Together with his professional team he strives to address and resolve the most difficult issues that arise on clients' projects.
